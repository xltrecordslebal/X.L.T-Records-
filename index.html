About Us

X.L.T Records is an independent record label and distribution platform founded by an artist who experienced firsthand the challenges of releasing music through unreliable services. After struggling with late releases, missing monetization, and poor support from major distributors, the vision for X.L.T Records was born — a label created by an artist, for artists.

We support a wide range of genres including Phonk, chill phonk, electronic, lo-fi, instrumental, and emotional-driven music. We believe in quality over quantity, transparency in reporting, and giving every artist the freedom to shape their sound without limits.
